About Our Company

Building Excellence in Glass & Structural Works

Lines & Columns Engineering Services was established in January 2019. Run and managed by a team of experienced professionals from various specialized expertise, we are committed to rendering state-of-the-art consultancy services in the fields of architecture, engineering, interior design, glass management, and other allied disciplines.

Operating in a professional environment and organized on modern lines, we are committed to providing high-quality services employing the current state of knowledge and contemporary practices. Our portfolio reflects this process with experience in the glass works supply and installation of several iconic projects. We have served and operated for clients nationwide. Lines & Columns Engineering Services' portfolio demonstrates our strong project delivery record and reflects our diversity of experience.

The Lines & Columns team consists of experienced employees. With years of successful practice, we have the capacity and experience to undertake a broad array of projects. Our efforts focus on getting great buildings built. We apply our experiences in design and documentation to all projects. We strive to introduce innovation and new concepts to invigorate our work continuously. This approach creates a unique quality for each glass plan and encourages continual growth of the company.
