Our Services

Lines and Columns Engineering Services provides comprehensive and integrated services at all stages of given planning in glass and structural works projects. These include preliminary and detailed financial and technical feasibility studies, project appraisal, and formulation of basic design concepts. At the detailed design stage, comprehensive designs are produced and drawings and specifications are developed. During the bidding phase services, comprise prequalification of contractors, preparation of contract documentation, bid soliciting, appraisal, and award of contract.

The services at the installation stage include contract administration, quality assurance. monitoring of schedules, verification of billing, attending to claims and disputes, and advice to the client on all aspects of his contractual liabilities and rights. The services offered include the evaluation of existing projects for upgrading and expansion. to establish continued fitness for the required purpose, or to study suitability for a proposed alternate use. Restoration, conservation, refurbishment, and modernization projects can be undertaken, and a complete range of services offered such as glass materials supplies.
